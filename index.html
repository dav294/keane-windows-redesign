Our Story

A family business
built on four decades
of trust

Keane Windows & Doors was founded in 1985 by Pat and Norah Keane as a customer-focused, family business with one simple mission — deliver exceptional windows and doors, and stand behind every single one of them.

Over the past 40 years, that mission hasn't changed. What started as a small operation out of Dublin has grown into one of Leinster's most trusted names in home glazing — serving families across Dublin, Meath, Kildare and Wicklow with the same personal care Pat brought to every job from day one.

Today, Pat is joined in the business by his son Pat Keane Jnr, bringing a second generation of expertise and the same hands-on approach that earned the family their reputation. When you call Keane Windows, you speak to a Keane — and that still means something.

The Problems We Fix

Sound familiar?

Every home has windows and doors. Not every home has good ones.

The Problem

Cold Drafts & Heat Loss

Old or poorly fitted windows leak heat constantly — and silently inflate your energy bills every month.

The Solution

Band A uPVC Windows

Our Koblenz uPVC windows achieve a Band A Window Energy Rating. High-performance double or triple glazing with weatherproof seals lock heat inside where it belongs.

The Problem

Tired, Dated Front Doors

Your front door is the first thing the world sees. An old, worn door drags down the entire look of your home.

The Solution

Composite Doors

The most secure, durable and energy-efficient external doors available. Six stunning colour options — White, Oak, Mahogany, Rosewood, Black and Cream — that transform your home instantly.

The Problem

Broken Locks & Stiff Hinges

A window that won't close properly or a lock that won't catch isn't just annoying — it's a security risk you live with every day.

The Solution

The Window Doctor

Our fully qualified Window Doctor fixes uPVC and aluminium windows fast. No callout charge. Free estimates. All work guaranteed. Covering Dublin, Meath, Kildare & Wicklow.
